CODING MINDS INTERNATIONAL SUMMIT 2015

The International Summit brings together the community of experts, academics, and activisits who comprising the creative Commons affiliate network in a different country every two year. This year, we hope to expand our inviation list including organisations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the Commons free culture and open knowledge. So if you're active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge - free software advocates, Wikipedia, Open knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundatiosn, lawyers, and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant commons together
